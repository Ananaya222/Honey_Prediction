Our Key Features

Purity Analysis

Advanced testing to detect adulteration in honey samples. Our system identifies common additives like sugar syrups, water, and other impurities.

Sugar Profile Analysis

Detect adulteration by analyzing sugar composition. Our system identifies unnatural sugar ratios (C3/C4 sugars) and common adulterants like corn syrup or inverted sugars.

Quality Grading System

Scientifically grade honey quality based on moisture content, HMF levels, diastase activity, and purity metrics. Get a clear A-F rating with improvement recommendations.

Quality Metrics

Detailed assessment of key quality indicators including moisture content, HMF levels, diastase activity, and more.

Food Nutrition Profile

Comprehensive analysis of calories, glycemic index, and functional nutrients. Perfect for dietary tracking and culinary applications.

Medicinal Properties

Analyze honey's therapeutic potential by measuring key biomarkers like antioxidant levels, hydrogen peroxide activity, and antibacterial potency for wound care and immune support.
